Your Sportsmen Information Connection

This website was created to help all deer hunters Oneida County. It will include links from the Wisconsin DNR Facebook page to help understand new rules and regulations. It should also help the public understand where the DNR is targeting their CWD testing efforts.

Your Oneida County Deer Advisory Council is asking for the public's support, please do your part and have your deer head tested. The DNR is hoping to get 450 deer heads submitted from the hunting public in 2018. It is fast, easy, and free. Currently there are four kiosks where you can do this located in Three Lakes, Woodruff, and Rhinelander as well as at Norling Taxidermy.

You can also authorize your local butcher to submit your deer head when you drop it off. Again, this process should be fast, easy, and free.
